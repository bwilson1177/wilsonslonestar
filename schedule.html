Before You Arrive

What to Know

  • → Bring a valid Texas ID to every class
  • → Arrive 10 minutes early — late arrivals may not be admitted
  • → Firearm courses: bring your own handgun (unloaded and cased) and required ammunition. Contact us if you need a loaner.
  • → Water and snacks recommended for longer sessions
  • → Wear comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing
  • → All participants must sign a liability waiver before class begins

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 72+ hours before class receive a full refund or credit toward a future session. Cancellations within 72 hours may be forfeited. If you have an emergency, please reach out — we'll work with you when we can.
