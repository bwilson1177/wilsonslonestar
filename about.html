Why Wilson's Lone Star Exists

Brent Wilson founded Wilson's Lone Star on a simple belief: the gap between owning a firearm and being truly prepared to use it is wider than most people realize. These courses exist to close that gap.

As a USCCA Certified Instructor, Brent brings decades of experience in personal defense and firearms instruction to every class. His teaching style is direct, patient, and always focused on real-world application over theory.

Brent and Wilson's Lone Star serve Waxahachie, Ellis County, and the broader DFW area — because every community deserves access to serious, trustworthy safety training.

Training Philosophy

What Makes Wilson's Lone Star Different

Every course at Wilson's Lone Star is built around one question: what would actually help you in an emergency? That means less range theater and more practical decision-making. Less fear, more confidence. And always — responsible, legal, and ethical use of force.

Students leave every class with skills they can explain to their family, practice at home, and carry with them every day.

Practical Over Theoretical

Every skill taught has a direct real-world application. No filler, no fluff.

Civilian Focused

These courses are built for families, homeowners, and everyday Texans — not operators.

Beginner Friendly

No prior experience assumed. No question too basic. Everyone starts somewhere.

Responsible & Legal

Texas law, ethical use of force, and safe handling are woven into every course.
