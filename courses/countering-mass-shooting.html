Course 04

Countering the Mass Shooting

Awareness-focused, action-oriented training for everyday civilians. Built around preparation — not fear.

A note on this course: This training is built around awareness and action — not fear. It's designed for the people who want to be prepared, not paralyzed. You will leave with practical skills and a clearer head, not anxiety.

About This Course

Active threat situations are unpredictable, but your response doesn't have to be. This course teaches civilians how to recognize warning signs early, make fast decisions under pressure, and take meaningful action — whether that means evacuating safely, sheltering effectively, or, for licensed carriers, making difficult use-of-force decisions.

This isn't tactical operator training. It's practical awareness and response skills built for the real world: your workplace, your church, your kids' school, your grocery store.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding the profile of active threat incidents in the U.S.
  • Pre-attack indicators: recognizing warning signs before shots are fired
  • Run-Hide-Fight framework: when and how to apply each option
  • Communicating with law enforcement during and after an incident
  • Protecting others in your care — staff, congregation, coworkers, children
  • For licensed carriers: decision-making under extreme stress
  • Post-incident steps: legal, medical, and psychological considerations

Who This Course Is For

  • General public — anyone who wants to feel more prepared in public spaces
  • Business owners and managers
  • Church leadership, security volunteers, and congregation members
  • School and university staff
  • Event and venue staff
  • Texas License to Carry holders seeking advanced situational awareness
