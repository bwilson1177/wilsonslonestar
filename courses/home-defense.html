Home Defense

Practical protection strategies for Texas families — built around real-world scenarios, not hypotheticals.

About This Course

Your home is your family's first line of defense. This course goes beyond simply owning a firearm — it teaches you how to think, plan, and act when a threat enters your space.

Built around real-world scenarios specific to Texas homes, this training is practical, scenario-based, and built for everyday people — not military operators.

What You'll Learn

  • How criminals choose targets — and how to make your home a harder one
  • Threat recognition and de-escalation principles
  • Safe room planning and family communication protocols
  • Low-light defensive techniques
  • Firearm storage that balances safety with accessibility
  • When and how to call 911 — what to say and what not to say
  • Post-incident steps if you've used force in your home

Who This Course Is For

  • Homeowners and renters in Waxahachie and the DFW area
  • Couples and families who want a shared safety plan
  • Concealed carriers who want to extend their skills to the home environment
  • Anyone who recently moved or has had a security concern
Attending as a couple or family is strongly encouraged. A home defense plan only works when everyone in the household knows it.
