Course 01

Firearm Training

Safe handling, marksmanship fundamentals, and responsible carry — for beginners and returning shooters alike.

About This Course

Whether you've never held a firearm or you're a returning shooter looking to sharpen your fundamentals, this course gives you the foundation to handle firearms safely and effectively.

Taught by a USCCA Certified Instructor, every session emphasizes safety first, legal responsibility, and the practical skills that carry over to real life — not just the range.

What You'll Learn

  • Safe storage, handling, and loading procedures
  • The four fundamental rules of firearm safety
  • Range etiquette and communication
  • Marksmanship basics: stance, grip, sight alignment, trigger control
  • Overview of Texas concealed carry law (License to Carry basics)
  • Situational awareness for armed citizens

Who This Course Is For

  • First-time gun owners
  • Anyone considering a Texas License to Carry (LTC)
  • Families adding a firearm to the home for the first time
  • Returning shooters who want to build confidence and correct habits
No prior experience required. Every skill is taught from the ground up. If you've never touched a firearm, this is the right starting point.
