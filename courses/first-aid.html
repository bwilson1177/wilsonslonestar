Course 03

First Aid Fundamentals

Life-saving civilian emergency skills — tourniquet application, wound packing, and emergency response. No medical background required.

About This Course

In an emergency, the actions you take in the first few minutes matter more than anything that comes after. This course gives you the practical skills to act with confidence — whether the emergency involves a firearm, a car accident, or a household injury.

No medical background required. This course is designed for civilians — parents, teachers, business owners, church volunteers, and anyone who wants to be genuinely prepared.

What You'll Learn

  • Tourniquet application — when and how to use one correctly
  • Wound packing and pressure techniques
  • Treating shock and controlling serious bleeding
  • Basic CPR orientation
  • Building a practical home and vehicle first aid kit
  • Calling 911: how to communicate clearly under stress
  • Triage basics for multi-person emergencies

Who This Course Is For

  • Anyone — this course does not require firearms ownership
  • Parents and caregivers
  • Teachers, coaches, and community volunteers
  • Business owners and workplace safety officers
  • Church and event staff
  • Firearm owners who want a complete safety picture
This course is built around Stop the Bleed methodology — one of the most effective civilian emergency response programs in the United States. You do not need to own or carry a firearm to benefit from this training.
