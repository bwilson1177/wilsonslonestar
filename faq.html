Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering for a course. Don't see your question? Send us a message.

Do I need a permit or license to take these courses?
No permit is required to enroll in any Wilson's Lone Star course. Texas law allows adults 18 and older to participate in firearms training. A License to Carry (LTC) is only required if you wish to legally carry a concealed handgun in Texas — and our Firearm Training course covers LTC basics to help you understand that process.
Are these courses appropriate for beginners?
Absolutely. Every Wilson's Lone Star course is designed to meet students where they are. No prior experience is assumed, and no question is too basic. Many students have never touched a firearm before their first class — that's exactly who these courses are built for.
What should I bring to class?
Requirements vary by course — check the individual course page for the full list. In general: bring a valid Texas ID, water, and comfortable clothing you can move in. For firearm courses, bring your own handgun (unloaded and cased) and the required ammunition. If you don't own a firearm, contact us before class.
Does Wilson's Lone Star provide firearms for training?
Loaner availability varies by session. Contact us before registering to ask about your specific class — we'll do our best to accommodate you.
What is the minimum age to attend?
Participants must be 18 years or older for most courses. Some courses may accommodate students aged 16–17 with written parental or guardian consent. Contact us to confirm requirements for a specific course.
Can I book a private class for my group, business, or church?
Yes. Private and group sessions are available for businesses, churches, HOAs, school staff, and families. These are some of our most popular bookings. Contact us to discuss scheduling and group pricing.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made more than 72 hours before class receive a full refund or credit toward a future session. Cancellations within 72 hours of class may be forfeited. If you have an emergency or extenuating circumstance, please reach out — we'll work with you when we can.
Is Wilson's Lone Star affiliated with USCCA?
The instructor is a USCCA Certified Instructor, which means training meets USCCA's standards for curriculum, instruction quality, and student safety. Wilson's Lone Star is an independent business and operates independently of USCCA as an organization.
What areas do you serve?
Wilson's Lone Star is based in Waxahachie, Texas and serves Ellis County and the greater DFW area — including Midlothian, Ennis, Corsicana, Mansfield, Cedar Hill, Red Oak, Glenn Heights, and surrounding communities.
Do I need to own a gun to attend the First Aid or Mass Shooting course?
No. First Aid Fundamentals and Countering the Mass Shooting are designed for all civilians — firearm ownership is not required or assumed. Anyone who wants practical emergency preparedness skills is welcome.
